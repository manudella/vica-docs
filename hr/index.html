    For Recruiters¶

    Welcome to VICA. This section covers everything you need to screen candidates, interpret reports, and validate VICA's accuracy.

    How VICA Works (30-Second Version)¶

    graph LR
    A["You upload CVs<br/>+ Job Description"] --> B["VICA reads<br/>every CV"]
    B --> C["AI identifies<br/>skills + evidence"]
    C --> D["Algorithm ranks<br/>candidates"]
    D --> E["You review reports<br/>with citations"]

    VICA does not make hiring decisions

    It provides a ranked shortlist with evidence. The final decision is always yours.

    Quick Reference: Recommendation Badges¶

    Badge Meaning Action
    Strong Yes Excellent fit across required skills Interview — high priority
    Yes Good fit, meets most requirements Interview — standard priority
    Maybe Mixed signals, gaps or unverified claims Review carefully
    No Missing critical required skills Deprioritize

    Quick Reference: Match Levels¶

    Level Meaning Badge Color
    Strong match Direct experience with real projects Green
    Partial match Some evidence but limited depth Yellow
    Transferable Related skill that could substitute Blue
    No match No evidence found Red
